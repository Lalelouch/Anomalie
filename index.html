ANOMALIE

Lara Bellatalla, Giorgia Bettineschi, Diletta D'Alto

An interaction between order and chaos, minimalist harmony dances with the glitch, revealing the anomaly in order.

An audio-visualization that blends minimalism and glitch in a simulated interface, reflecting the chaotic, essential, and reduced approach of musical composition.

Click "play/stop" to start and stop the video, move the bar to raise or lower the volume, move the timeline bar to go forward or backward.

Music: "UNIEQAV #02 UNI MIA" by ALVA NOTO
